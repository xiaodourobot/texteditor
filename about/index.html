About TextEditor

Text Editor is a free online tool collection for generating and converting text.
Find here in one place everything that font-related: enojis, characters, ASCII art and more.

We didn't invent anything new, we just collected other individual websites in one place and made them even better.
Or at least we hope that our visitors find these tools useful as well as we do.
Our plan is to add even more features to Text Editor in the future.
Our ultimate goal is becoming the best and most popular text converter.

Text Paint

Our other project is Text Paint, an online drawing program which allows you to paint characters on a canvas.

It works like any other paint program but instead of pixels and colors here we can add characters to the canvas and export the result into a text file.

Visit textpaint.luhui.net to give it a try.
